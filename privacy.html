1. Overview

TahminEtNeOlacak ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our website, automation tools, and social media channels.

This Service is operated by a single creator for personal, non-commercial content publishing. We do not operate a public user registration system.

2. Information We Collect

Website visitors: We do not use analytics or advertising trackers on this site.

OAuth integrations: When connecting social media accounts (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram), we obtain access tokens authorized by the account owner. These tokens are stored locally on the operator's machine and are not sold or shared with third parties.

Social media platforms: When you interact with our content on YouTube, Instagram, or TikTok, those platforms may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

3. How We Use Information

  • To publish videos to authorized social media accounts
  • To manage OAuth authentication with platform APIs
  • To operate and maintain the content pipeline

We do not use data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following types of third-party services:

  • Social platforms: YouTube, Instagram, TikTok — for publishing content
  • AI & media APIs: Anthropic, Google (Gemini/Veo), stock video providers — for content creation
  • Hosting: Netlify — to serve this website

Each provider has its own privacy policy governing their handling of data.

5. Data Retention

OAuth tokens are retained only as long as needed for publishing automation. Video project files are stored locally by the operator and deleted according to operational needs.

6. Security

API credentials and tokens are stored in environment variables on the operator's local system and are not committed to public repositories.

7. Your Rights

If you have questions about data we hold, or wish to revoke platform access, you can disconnect the TahminEtNeOlacak app from your TikTok, Google, or Meta account settings at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

Our content is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 through this website.

9. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the latest revision.

10. Contact
